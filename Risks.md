## 3. Balance Concerns (Important)

### 3.1 Free trading can break the game

Because:

* Trades are unlimited
* Everything can be traded (even workers, cities, settlements)
* No binding enforcement except â€œagreementâ€

**Risks:**

* Kingmaking
* One player feeding another to victory
* Two-player alliances dominating others

**Tips:**

* Consider **non-transferable items** (e.g. workers, cities)
* Or add **trade limits per turn**
* Or introduce **reputation / trust penalties** for extreme trades

---

### 3.2 Maintenance costs are brutal (maybe too brutal)

Worker cost:

```
Worker Level + Distance + Roads
```

This can snowball into:

* Players being forced to delete workers
* Rich-get-richer via cities

**Tip:**

* Cap distance cost (e.g. max +3)
* Or let roads reduce maintenance, not increase it

---

### 3.3 Domination victory seems rare

> â€œAll enemy workers have been destroyed.â€

Given:

* Workers downgrade instead of dying
* Combat is costly to attacker
* Workers are limited but valuable

**Tip:**
This victory condition may almost never happen.

* Either add **alternative military victory**
* Or allow capturing instead of only downgrading
